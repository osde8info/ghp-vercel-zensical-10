how to use goog #translate to make #vietnamese iced coffee

- buy some instant vietnamese coffee with vietnamese instructions
- download google translate
- choose vietnamese to english
- point phone camera at coffee packet
- take photo
- highlight instructions with finger
- make iced coffee
